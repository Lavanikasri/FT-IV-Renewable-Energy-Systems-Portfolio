Explanation

The solar-powered irrigation pumping system harnesses solar radiation to extract water for farming. The solar PV array converts sunlight into DC electricity, optimized through an MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) controller to ensure efficient energy transfer. This powers a submersible pump that draws water from a borewell and directs it to a storage tank or irrigation field. The system operates off-grid, removing the need for diesel or grid-based electricity, making it sustainable and cost-effective. It supports eco-friendly agriculture, improves irrigation reliability, and contributes to rural energy independence.
